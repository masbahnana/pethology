Getting Started

What is Pethology?

Pethology is a comprehensive learning platform designed specifically for veterinary students and teachers. It provides interactive quizzes, progress tracking, achievements, and study tools to help students master veterinary concepts and prepare for practical assessments.

The platform includes features like adaptive quizzes, skills demonstrations, glossary management, and detailed progress analytics.

How do I create an account?

Pethology uses a whitelist system to ensure only authorized users can access the platform:

  • Students: Your teacher must add you to the student whitelist before you can create an account
  • Teachers: You must be added to the teacher whitelist by an administrator

Once you're whitelisted, simply click "Login" and follow the authentication process through Auth0.

Is Pethology free to use?

Yes! Pethology is currently completely free for students and teachers. Our goal is to support veterinary education and make quality learning tools accessible to everyone.

What devices can I use Pethology on?

Pethology works on any device with a modern web browser:

  • Desktop: Full-featured experience on Windows, Mac, or Linux
  • Tablet: Touch-optimized interface for iPad and Android tablets
  • Mobile: Responsive design works on all smartphones

We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge for the best experience.

Do I need to install anything?

No! Pethology is a web-based application. Just open your browser, navigate to the website, and log in. No downloads or installations required.

Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud, so you can access it from any device.

For Students

How do quizzes work?

Pethology offers two types of quizzes:

  • Standard Quizzes: Pre-made quizzes covering all 10 veterinary modules (200+ questions)
  • Custom Quizzes: Created by your teacher with optional deadlines

Our adaptive quiz system intelligently adjusts question difficulty based on your performance, ensuring you're always learning at the right level.

What are achievements and how do I unlock them?

Achievements are rewards for reaching milestones in your learning journey. There are 19 different achievements to unlock:

  • Quiz Achievements: Complete specific numbers of quizzes (5, 10, 25, 50, 100)
  • Score Achievements: Reach certain average scores (70%, 80%, 90%)
  • Module Achievements: Master specific veterinary modules
  • Special Achievements: Perfect scores, speed challenges, and more

Check your "My Achievements" page to see your progress toward each achievement.

How do Skills Demonstrations work?

Skills Demonstrations are practical checklists created by your teacher to help you prepare for hands-on assessments:

  • View published demonstrations in your dashboard
  • Check off tasks as you complete them
  • Track your progress with visual indicators
  • Mark yourself as "Ready for Assessment" when you're 100% complete

Your teacher can see your progress in real-time to know when you're ready for evaluation.

What is the Glossary and how do I use it?

The Glossary is a comprehensive dictionary of veterinary terms managed by your teacher. It includes:

  • Terms organized by category (Anatomy, Diseases, Procedures, Tools, Medications)
  • Search functionality to quickly find definitions
  • Multiple view modes (A-Z, by category, grid view)

Use it as a quick reference while studying or taking quizzes.

Can my teacher see my quiz results?

Yes. Your teacher can view your progress through the Class Management system, including:

  • Quiz scores and completion rates
  • Average scores and trends
  • Weak topics that need improvement
  • Quiz history and timeline

This helps your teacher provide targeted support and identify areas where you might need extra help.

How is my progress tracked?

Your progress is automatically saved after every quiz. You can view detailed analytics in your "My Progress" page:

  • Overall Stats: Total quizzes, average score, total time spent
  • Module Breakdown: Performance by topic with visual charts
  • Quiz History: Complete timeline of all attempts with scores and topics
  • Weak Topics: Areas identified for improvement based on your performance

For Teachers

How do I add students to my class?

You can add students in two ways:

  • Manual Entry: Click "Import Students" and add students one by one with their name and email
  • CSV Upload: Upload a CSV file with columns for name and email to add multiple students at once

Once added to the whitelist, students can create their accounts and start learning.

Can I create my own quizzes?

Yes! Use the Custom Quiz Import system to create your own quizzes:

  • Upload questions via CSV file
  • Add quiz title, topic, and difficulty
  • Set optional deadlines for assignments
  • Quizzes appear automatically in your students' dashboards

This is perfect for assignments, homework, or topic-specific assessments.

How do I create Skills Demonstrations?

Navigate to "Skills Demos" from your dashboard and click "Create New Demo":

  • Add title and description
  • Create checklist items with task descriptions and completion criteria
  • Save as Draft (only you can see) or Publish (visible to students)
  • View student progress to see who's ready for assessment

You can edit or delete demos at any time.

How do I manage the Glossary?

Access the Glossary management from your dashboard to:

  • Add new terms with definitions and categories
  • Edit existing terms
  • Delete outdated terms
  • Search and filter by category
  • View stats (total terms by category)

All changes are instantly visible to your students.

Can I make announcements to my class?

Yes! Use the Announcements system to communicate with your students:

  • Create announcements that appear on student dashboards
  • Add titles and detailed messages
  • Edit or delete announcements as needed
  • Students see announcements immediately upon login

Perfect for reminders about deadlines, exam dates, or important notices.

Technical

Is my data secure?

Yes. Pethology uses industry-standard security practices:

  • Auth0: Enterprise-grade authentication with encrypted login
  • Firebase: Google Cloud infrastructure for data storage
  • Whitelist System: Only authorized users can access the platform
  • HTTPS: All data is encrypted in transit

Your personal information and quiz data are never shared with third parties.

What browsers are supported?

Pethology works best on modern browsers:

  • Recommended: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (latest versions)
  • Mobile: Safari (iOS), Chrome (Android)

We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for optimal performance and security.

Can I use Pethology offline?

Currently, Pethology requires an internet connection to function. Offline support is planned for a future release.

Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud, so you can pick up where you left off on any device.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We're continuously improving Pethology and appreciate bug reports. Please contact your teacher with the following information:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (1, 2, 3...)
  • Your browser and device type
  • Screenshots if applicable

Your teacher can forward the report to the development team for investigation.

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact your teacher for additional support.
