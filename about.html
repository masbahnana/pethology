About Pethology

Our Mission

Pethology was born out of a passion for animal care and a desire to make learning more engaging for students. We offer a platform where individuals can access high-quality study resources tailored to the QQI Level 5 Animal Care course, designed to help you succeed in the world of animal welfare and healthcare.

What We Offer

Our platform includes detailed course content, interactive quizzes, and blog posts created by fellow students and professionals. Whether you're studying biology, animal behavior, or welfare, Pethology brings all the resources you need to one place.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate animal care students and professionals who aim to bring the best of our knowledge to the community. Our goal is to make animal care education more accessible, engaging, and practical.
