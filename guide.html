I built Pethology because I know how hard it can be to study for Animal Care exams without the right tools. I wanted to create something that actually helps — not just a pile of notes, but an interactive platform where you can test yourself, track your progress, and feel confident going into exams.

I'm a student too (or was recently!), so I built this with a student's perspective. Everything here was designed to make your studying easier, more engaging, and less stressful.

🎓 Student Guide

1

Log in for the first time

Your teacher has already added you to the platform using your school email. Go to pethology.netlify.app, click 🐾 Log in in the top right, and sign in with that same email. You'll be taken straight to your Student Dashboard — no sign-up needed.

💡 Make sure to use the same email your teacher registered you with, otherwise the system won't recognise you.
2

Take your first quiz

From your dashboard, click Start Quiz or go directly to the Quiz page. Choose a module — like Animal Anatomy, Grooming, or Animal Welfare — and start answering. You'll get instant feedback on each question.

💡 Try to complete at least one quiz per module to unlock Smart Review!
3

Use Smart Review

Smart Review looks at your quiz history and automatically picks your weakest module to focus on. Click Start Review on your dashboard and it will launch a quiz on the topic you need the most practice with.

4

Try Exam Mode

When you feel ready, try Exam Mode. It simulates real exam conditions — a timer, no hints, and no feedback until the end. It's a great way to test yourself before the real thing.

⏱ The timer is automatically adjusted based on how fast you've been answering in past quizzes.
5

Track your progress

Your dashboard shows your total quizzes completed, average score, current streak and achievements. Head to My Progress in the sidebar to see a detailed breakdown by module.

6

Unlock achievements

As you study, you'll unlock achievements — from First Steps (complete your first quiz) to Brain Master (complete all 10 modules). Check the Achievements page to see what's waiting for you!

📋 Teacher Guide

1

Log in to your account

Go to pethology.netlify.app and click 🐾 Log in in the top right. Use your school email address. Since your email is on the approved teacher list, you'll be automatically redirected to the Teacher Dashboard.

💡 If you see a "Student Dashboard" instead of a Teacher one, contact Nana — your email may need to be added to the teacher list.
2

Create your first class

On your first login, a prompt will appear asking if you'd like to create a class. Click Yes. A form will open — enter your class name (e.g. "Animal Care Year 1") and the academic year. Click Create Class to confirm.

💡 You can create multiple classes if you teach more than one group.
3

Add your students

Go to the Settings tab in the Teacher Dashboard. You'll see a student list for your class. Click + Add Student and enter each student's name and school email address. Do this for every student in your class.

Once added, students can log in with that email and their progress will be automatically linked to your class.

⚠️ Make sure to use the exact email address the student will log in with — it's how the system recognises them.
4

Share the student guide with your class

Send your students this link so they can get started: pethology.netlify.app/guide.html. It walks them through logging in, taking quizzes, and using all the features. The platform works on phones too, so they can study anywhere.

5

Monitor your students' progress

Back on the main dashboard, you'll see all your students listed with their quiz count, average score, and last activity. Click on any student to see their full breakdown by module — which topics they're doing well in and which ones need more attention.

💡 Use this to focus class time on the modules where students are struggling the most.
6

Check the class overview

The top of your dashboard shows overall class stats — total quizzes completed, class average score, and active students. A quick way to see how engaged your class is at a glance.
