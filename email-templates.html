Email Best Practices

📧 Tips for Successful Invitations:

  • Personalize: Always use the recipient's name
  • Be clear: Explain what Pethology is in simple terms
  • Highlight benefits: Focus on how it helps THEM (not features)
  • Include links: Make it easy to access (for-teachers.html, for-students.html)
  • Provide support: Let them know how to get help
  • Follow up: Send a reminder after 3-4 days to non-responders
